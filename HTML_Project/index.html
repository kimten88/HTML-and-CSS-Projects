Common types of tea

Green Tea

Green tea is one of the most common and recognizable tpyes of tea. Its pale color resembles the color of the green camillia sensis leaf. It is high caffiene but also has high levels a thianine which gives this type of popular tea a unique burst of calm energy.

Black Tea

Black tea is the most consumed type of tea world wide. This tea leaf is fully oxidized and gives a much darker color. Due to this tea's redish color after the brewing process it is known as red tea in China.

Oolong Tea

This type of tea is partially oxidized. It is popular mostly in China and Taiwan. Oolong can also be spelt Wulong. There are more varieties due to the open ended nature of it being paritally oxidized.
